Plan Your Solar Future

Navigating the financial landscape of domestic solar power isn’t easy. There are many factors to consider: projected solar production, future electricity costs, payment options, interest rates, and more. The calculations can become so complex that many people simply give up.

This is a mistake. Domestic solar power can make a lot of sense. Beyond the environmental benefits, it can deliver substantial long-term savings and increase your home’s value. Walking away from solar could mean missing out on significant financial returns.

Of course, no one wants to make a major investment hastily. Smart homeowners want the facts — only then can they decide whether solar power is right for their home.

That’s where SunWise comes in. The SunWise Solar Calculator is a free, comprehensive, independent tool that helps you estimate the likely financial benefits of investing in solar power.
