Solar in Arizona - Harness the Sunshine for Maximum Savings

Arizona enjoys some of the highest solar potential in the country. The bright, consistent sunlight can significantly reduce your electricity bills all year round. Moreover, solar systems add long-term value to your home, as Arizona home buyers are on the lookout for homes equipped with solar power.

Peak Solar Output in Hot Summers

Arizona’s climate features long, sunny days with minimal cloud cover, creating optimal conditions for solar panels. Peak energy demand from AC coincides with peak power production, meaning that solar power directly offsets the most expensive electricity consumption. Even during milder winter months, solar systems continue to generate significant energy, reducing your daytime utility bills.

Spacious Homes and Roofs Make Installation Easier

Many homes in Arizona have large footprints and expansive rooftops, offering plenty of space for solar panels. A well-designed system, with optimum roof orientation and tilt, can enhance efficiency to generate huge savings.

Financial Incentives That Make Solar More Accessible

Arizona homeowners enjoy numerous additional financial incentives, including a Solar Tax Credit, Sales Tax Exemption, and Property Tax Exemption on the additional property value arising from your solar installation. In addition, Net Metering allows homeowners to receive financial credit for excess solar power sent to the grid.

These benefits can add up to thousands of dollars, and are among the many factors leading tens of thousands of Arizona homeowners each year to adopt solar power.
